## Slide 2: The Problem
**Challenge**: 
University students often struggle with:
- Managing multiple courses and deadlines.
- Tracking study hours effectively.
- Communicating efficiently with peers and professors.
- Staying motivated without clear progress visualization.

**Users**: Primary focus on University Students, with administrative oversight capabilities.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Overview**: 
A comprehensive mobile application built with **Flutter** that creates a digital ecosystem for academic success.
- **Centralized**: All courses, exams, and notes in one place.
- **Interactive**: Real-time chat for doubt resolution.
- **Smart**: Built-in timers and progress tracking.
- **Secure**: Authentication with role-based access control.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features (Student Tools)
1.  **Course Management**: 
    - Detailed view of all enrolled courses.
    - Track credits, professor details, and schedules.
2.  **Study Sessions & Timer**:
    - Dedicated Focus Timer for deep work.
    - History of study sessions to analyze productivity.
3.  **Performance Tracking**:
    - Visual charts (fl_chart) showing study trends.
    - GPA and credit calculation.

---

## Slide 5: Key Features (Collaboration & Admin)
1.  **Real-Time Chat**:
    - Integrated Socket.IO chat for instant messaging.
    - Connect with professors and study groups.
2.  **Admin Dashboard**:
    - Manage users and professors.
    - System-wide statistics and oversight.
    - Specific tools for academic administrators.

---

## Slide 6: Technical Architecture
**Frontend**: 
- **Framework**: Flutter (Dart) - Cross-platform performance.
- **State Management**: Provider Pattern - Efficient app-wide state sharing.
- **UI Kit**: Custom Material Design with Light/Dark mode support.

**Technology Stack**:
- **Persistence**: `shared_preferences` for local data.
- **Charts**: `fl_chart` for data visualization.
- **Real-time**: `socket_io_client` for messaging.
- **Notifications**: `flutter_local_notifications` for reminders.

---

## Slide 7: User Experience (UX)
- **Theming**: Dynamic Light and Dark themes based on user preference.
- **Localization**: Built from the ground up to support multiple languages (EN, ES, IT, etc.).
- **Responsive Navigation**: Intuitive flow with custom transitions and splash screens.
- **Safety**: Secure login flow with token-based authentication.
